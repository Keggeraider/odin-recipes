Lasagne Recipe

Description

This is an authentic lasagne bolgonese recipe from Italy.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne noodles
  • Besciamella
  • Bolognese Sauce
    • Ground beef (300g)
    • Pancetta (150g)
    • Carrots (50g)
    • Celery (50g)
    • Yellow onion (50g)
    • Red wine (100g)
    • Tomato puree (300g)
    • Vegetable broth (to taste)
    • Extra virgin olive oil (1 tbsp)
    • Fine salt (to taste)
    • Black pepper (to taste)
