Lasagne Recipe

Description

This is an authentic lasagne bolgonese recipe from Italy.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne noodles
  • Besciamella
  • Bolognese Sauce
    • Ground beef (300g)
    • Pancetta (150g)
    • Carrots (50g)
    • Celery (50g)
    • Yellow onion (50g)
    • Red wine (100g)
    • Tomato puree (300g)
    • Vegetable broth (to taste)
    • Extra virgin olive oil (1 tbsp)
    • Fine salt (to taste)
    • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Parmigiano reggiano (270g)
  • Butter (to taste)
  1. Heat the vegetable broth in a seperate pan on low and keep warm.
  2. Chop the pancetta well.
  3. Gather the carrots, onion, celery and finely chop and set aside.
  4. In a saucepan, add Olive oil and pancetta.
  5. Brown the pancetta for a few minutes on medium-low heat.
  6. Add the finely chopped veggies that were set aside earlier to the pancetta and let cook for 5-6 minutes.
  7. Stir in the ground beef, raise the heat to medium, and brown slowly.
  8. Once the ground beef is fully cooked, pour in the red wine and deglaze the pan
  9. Once the red wine is completely cooked down and evaporated, add the tomato puree.
  10. When the sauce stars boiling, stir in one or two ladles of the hot vegetable broth and let the sauce simmer for at least a couple of hours covered with the lid open slightly to vent.
  11. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to tase.
  12. Grease the bottom of a large lasagna pan.
  13. Add a thin layer of the besciamella sauce.
  14. Add a thin layer of the bolognese sauce.
  15. Place one or more sheets of the lasagne noodles to form a single layer above the sauce.
  16. Add a second layer of besciamella sauce.
  17. Add a second layer of bolognese sauce.
  18. Add a some of the grated parmigiano reggiano.
  19. Add another layer of lasagne noodles to create a single layer on top of the parmigiano.
  20. Add a third layer of besciamella sauce.
  21. Add a third layer of bolognese sauce.
  22. Add some more parmigiano on the bolognese sauce.
  23. Add another layer of lasagne noodles.
  24. Continue in this way until you have 5 layers stacked.
  25. To the top of the final lasagne noodle, add a layer of the bolognese sauce completely covering the noodle.
  26. Sprinkle parmigiano reggiano on the bolognese sauce.
  27. If desired, add some slices of butter.
  28. Bake in a preheated oven at 350 F for 40 minutes.
