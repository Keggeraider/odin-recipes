Bread Recipe

Description

This is an easy beginner bread recipe.

Ingredients

  • Unbleached bread flour (540g)
  • Granulated sugar (11g)
  • Table salt (15g)
  • Lukewarm water (379g)

Steps

  1. Stir all of the ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Mix everything with a wooden spoon until it's well mixed into a shaggy ball.
  3. Sprinkle some flour onto your working surface and gently remove the dough from the bowl to the floured surface.
  4. Knead the dough for approximately 10 minutes or until the ball has a smooth surface.
  5. Place the ball in a lightly greased bowl.
  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a damp towel.
  7. Let the dough rise at room temperature until it has doubled in size.
  8. Remove the dough from the bowl to the lightly floured surface.
  9. Gently deflate the dough and cut the dough in half.
  10. Shape each dough into loaves and let rise on floured surface, covered by a damp towl for 1 hour.
  11. Place the loaves on a parchment lined baking sheet.
  12. Slice a 1/4" deep line down each loaf.
  13. Bake the loaves in a 450 F preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  14. Remove the loaves from the oven and allow to cool for atleast 1 hour before cutting.
